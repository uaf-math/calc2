\problem{8} Let $R$ be the region bounded by $y=6x-3x^2$. Use the Method of Cylindrical Shells to find the volume of the solid obtain by rotating $R$ about the $y$-axis.  (Hint: Sketch $R.$ Sketch a sample slice of $R$.) \vfill

\problem{4} Let $R$ be the region bounded by $x=\sqrt{y}+1$, $x=1$, and $x=3$. Use the Method of Cylindrical Shells to \emph{set up but do not evaluate} an integral to find the volume of the solid obtain by rotating $R$ about the $x$-axis.

\problem{4} \emph{Set up but do not evaluate} an integral for the length of the curve $y=\sin(x)$ from $x=0$ to $x=\pi.$ \\
