\problem{5} Sketch the graph defined by the parametric equations below and eliminate the parameter. (You can do these two tasks in whatever order you like.) Your sketch should include direction and have at least two points labelled.\\

$x(t)=\sqrt{t}, \: y(t)=2t+4$

\vfill
\problem{6} Sketch the graph defined by the parametric equations below and eliminate the parameter. (You can do these two tasks in whatever order you like.) Your sketch should include direction and should have at least \textbf{four} points labelled.\\

$x(t)=2\cos(t), \: y(t)=4+\sin(t)$
\vfill
