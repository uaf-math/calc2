\item Definition: The $n$-th Taylor \emph{polynomial} of $f(x)$ centered at $x=a$ is: 

$$\ds p_n(x)= \sum_{k=0}^n \frac{f^{(k)}(a)}{k!}(x-a)^k=f(a)+f'(a)(x-a)+\frac{f''(a)}{2!}(x-a)^2+\frac{f'''(a)}{3!}(x-a)^3+\cdots+\frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n.$$
